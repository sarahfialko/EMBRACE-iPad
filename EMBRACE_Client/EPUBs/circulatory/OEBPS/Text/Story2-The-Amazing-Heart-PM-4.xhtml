The blood rushes out and into the red tube that goes to the arm and other parts of the body. Tubes that take blood from the heart to the body are called arteries. When the left ventricle squeezes hard it makes the heart beat. Sometimes you can feel your heart beat by putting your hand on your chest. Your heart beats around 80 to 100 times each minute. Heartbeats tell us that a person is alive and pumping blood to the body.
